Performance, video

4.48 am, i can't just wait

2020

What can I do if all I want is for you to be here
or for me to be there?



This performance is not about an expression of sadness but a way for me to feel better. Because materializing things makes them clear. And ‘doing’ is what I really needed after all. And to realize that I am not stopped or still.
