Video, action research

i don't need the adobe® suite

2020

I am interested in which systems we are using and how. How do we use, get and share informations in the past and today? What are the effects on our work, progress, relationships? This project came to life after a research on open-source and free software values and applications (e.g the personal control, customizability and freedom it can provide).

I experimented within the domain of graphic design and video making, trying to create fully analog visuals that would seem digitally made.
