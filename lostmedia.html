Collaboration, editorial, archive, photography

lost media

A collaborative project with Ward Lauwers and Toshihito Endo

2021

This publication lays out a conversation with the Werker Collective about amateur photograpy movements. We juxtaposed this text to forgotten warm media we found in the street and flea markets. Arranging these photographs from different origins and type (roll films, VHS tapes, SD cards), we let them speak with each other and interact with the words for it to be even more resonating.
