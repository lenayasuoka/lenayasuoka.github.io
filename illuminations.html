early digital illuminations

2021

Early Digital Illuminations is a project celebrating the digital folklore and visual culture born from prior use of the personal computer. It blends the 3 temporalities of ascii art, GIFs and 3D modeled visuals.

It takes inspirations from illuminations and baroque ornemented letters. The alphabet plays with the concept of low resolution and looks for what information is lost and gained, along with the quest for smallest file size stil visually efficient.

Eventually, the project involved a research into several visual techniques and how the medium affects the content through; 1 alphabet making, 2 silkscreen printing, 3 animation (using Processing software) and 4 printed publication.
