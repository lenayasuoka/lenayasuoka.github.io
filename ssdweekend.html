ssd weekend

A collaborative project with Ward Lauwers and ASML company

2020

This project dives into the topic of electronic and packaging waste of a big tech company; parts ranging from cardboard to twenty thousand euros specialized components.

Confronted by these massive logistical and economical systems, we had to shift our perception and show pro- positions the company would never expect. These materials therefore became elements of broader narratives, unraveling their symbolic potential and embodying a serious absurdity.


The first research phase ended up in a publication and a platform curating informative and experimental findings. It brought together large topics of world scale wasted technology from the company and more intimate relation of humans, in houses and gardens, far away from clean rooms.
